Postcard Generator

Keeping in touch with family and friends is important, espeically when we are busy and stressed. In this assignment for ECS 162: Web Programming, a postcard generator app was made that lets a user select a background color, upload a photo, and choose a font style to create a unique postcard that they can send to their loved ones.
